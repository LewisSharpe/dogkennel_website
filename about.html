About Us

The Kennels Company is a long-established kennels and grooming service in Rosyth Fife.

We have kennels and chalets to house all sizes, shapes and breeds of all dogs. Whether your dog is a Chihuahua or a Great Dane our experienced and dedicated staff will provide the best possible care for them.

If you have any questions or comments then please feel free to contact us for more information!

You can phone us on (01383) 418654 or email us at info@thekennels.com
