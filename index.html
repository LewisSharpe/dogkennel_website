The Kennels Company Ltd.

The Kennels Company was established in 1998 and has providing kennel and grooming services within the local company for almost 24 years now.

Our small boarding kennels based in Rosyth has 16 individual luxury kennels which allow us to provide a quality environment for your much loved dogs.

We also provide grooming services for dogs that include shampooing, haircuts and cutting toenails.

We conduct a collection service for additional charge to collect and groom your dog at our facility. For this service, we serve Rosyth, Inverkeithing, Aberdour, Dalgety Bay, Limekilns, Charleston, Dunfermline and South Queensferry.

Reasons for choosing us:

  1. Spacious kennels for your dog,

  2. An indoor heating within the kennels for your dog,

  3. An outdoor covered area for your dog to play in,

  4. Highly experienced staff with a wide range of dog

  5. Individual care and assistance personalised to meet your dogs needs, and,

  6. Your dog will be fed the finest quality diet while at our facility
